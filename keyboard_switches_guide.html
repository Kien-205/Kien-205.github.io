The Complete Guide to Mechanical Switches

The switch is the soul of the mechanical keyboard. Whether you want a silent office board or a "thocky" gaming setup, it all starts here.

01. Know the Lingo

TermDefinition
Actuation ForceHow hard you need to press (measured in grams/gf).
Bottom-outForce required to press the key all the way down.

02. The Three Main Types

Linear

Smooth consistent keystroke. Best for Gaming.

Tactile

Noticeable "bump" in the middle. Best for Typing.

Clicky

Tactile bump with a sharp audible "click".
