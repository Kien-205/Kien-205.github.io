The Ultimate Guide to Keycaps

Keycaps affect the sound, feel, and aesthetics of your build more than any other component.

01. Comparison: ABS vs PBT

ABS Plastic

Standard for vibrant sets (GMK).

  • Feel: Smooth, slick.
  • Sound: "Clackier".
  • Cons: Shines over time.

PBT Plastic

Known for durability.

  • Feel: Textured, dry.
  • Sound: "Thockier".
  • Pros: Never shines.

02. Keycap Profiles

Cherry Profile

The gold standard. Cylindrical, low height.

OEM Profile

Taller than Cherry. Standard on pre-builts.

SA Profile

Tall, spherical, retro. Deep thock sound.

XDA / DSA

Flat and uniform height.
