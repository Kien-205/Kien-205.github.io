Why Go Mechanical?

Most keyboards are just plastic sheets. A mechanical keyboard uses individual switches for every key. It's a simple upgrade that changes how you interact with your computer every day.

1. Better Feel

Standard keyboards feel mushy. Mechanical switches feel crisp and responsive.

2. Custom Sound

You can choose how your keyboard sounds. Do you want the loud "click" of a typewriter? Or the deep, quiet "thock"?

3. Personal Style

Keyboards don't have to be boring black rectangles. With custom keycaps, you can match your keyboard to your room.
